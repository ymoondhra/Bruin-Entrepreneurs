get involved

We hold our weekly meetings at Startup UCLA on Tuesdays at 7:30. Through our newsletter, we provide weekly content about events that we host, weekly meetings, entrepreneur-related events, and even opportunities for internships with startups around L.A.
Join our Facebook group and email newsletter to stay updated!

join board!

If you're excited about being part of running the largest student-run group tackling the LA start-up scene, apply to be part of the Bruin Entrepreneurs Board! We love people who are passionate to contribute. We need people across a range of skill sets, starting from design, marketing, event planning, partnerships, and many more.
Applications open again Fall 2019!
